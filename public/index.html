System Synchronization Overview

This system connects to the Variety Vintage Performance Intelligence Portal (https://varietyvintage.com/employee) to pull live attendance, leave approvals, and employee credentials. Monthly salary structures can be loaded below using CSV or Excel formats to prepare payslip disbursements.

Salary Spreadsheet Instructions

To ensure proper payslip generation, format your spreadsheet with the following columns:

  • username or employee_name - Matches synced employee profiles (e.g., ksheera, gani or Santhosh B).
  • base_salary or salary - The monthly base pay (e.g., 42000).
  • allowance (Optional) - Special allowances or bonuses.
  • pf (Optional) - Provident Fund deductions.
  • tds (Optional) - Tax deductions.

The system will automatically link base salaries, deduct ₹250 for each logged late arrival, and calculate Loss of Pay (LOP) days proportionally based on calendar days in the selected month.
